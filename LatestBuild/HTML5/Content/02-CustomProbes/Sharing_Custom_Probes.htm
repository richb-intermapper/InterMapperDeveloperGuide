Sharing Probes

InterMapper has an enthusiastic user-base that has contributed literally hundreds of probes, many of which have been adopted as built-in probes. Users are encouraged to check out the library of user-contributed probes, and to contribute useful probes themselves.

Sharing Your Own Probes

If you create a probe you find useful, please contribute your new probe so others can use it as well. Send us an e-mail at support@intermapper.com and we'll post it to the Contributions page mentioned above.

Using Contributed Probes

You can use any of the probes that Help/Systems has created, or use probes contributed by our other customers. These probes are available from:

http://intermapper.com/go.php?to=probes.contrib
